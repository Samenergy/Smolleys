Welcome to Smolleys

Teaching children the ABCs of money management in a fun and engaging way!

Download the 'Smolleys (Digital Banking For Kids)' app from the Play Store or App Store, create an account, and embark on a fun and educational financial journey with your child. Start teaching them the essentials of money management today!

Features

See what Smolleys can offer your family

Kids Savings

Empower children to save, learn, and grow. Smolleys provides a safe, interactive space for kids to set savings goals, make deposits, and track their financial progress. Encourage responsible saving habits from a young age, nurturing a lifelong understanding of financial literacy

Money Quizzes

Test financial knowledge with interactive quizzes. Challenge kids to grasp budgeting, saving, and spending concepts in a playful and informative way. Track progress and reward success as they become financial literate

Kids do tasks or Chores

Empower kids to learn responsibility and financial skills by assigning tasks or chores within Smolleys. They can complete tasks, earn virtual allowances, and gain valuable insights into the connection between effort and reward, fostering a sense of accountability and financial literacy.

Book Store

An online book store is a feature offering a diverse range of books, including e-books and audiobooks. Users can browse, purchase, and access a wide selection of titles, read book details, reviews, and ratings, and enjoy features like wishlists, recommendations, and cloud storage for a convenient and immersive reading experience.
